how to play:

to start game press m, will be used to pause game
usage: wsad or arrow keys to move,
space to shoot
e to switch weapons
f to upgrade weapon, currently unlimited, will use cost of resources eventually

initial upgrade cost per weapon, assume cost doubles after each upgrade
ammo:200, overall decent weapon, quick shooting, ok damage
laser:500 goes through non rock enemies and does major damage, but doesnt do well against rocks, worst shoot rate
missile:350 best against rocks, but slow initial shooting

enemies:
spammer: move in programmed movement, generally weak
follower:moves to hover above player, stronger than spammer
asteroid: circle rock that floats through space, avoid collision, may contain worm
enemy inside of worm, small chance of spawning, must destroy rock before you can damage worm
rotates to player location, once free goes towards player

land on comets, semitransparent rock, to mine for resources, movement is limited to left and right,
press x to disengage from comet when bar is passed the green portion
get rewarded with health when disengaging, but stay on the comet too long at it may explode and hurt you
every next comet should give more resources
otherwise killenemies to gain resources
